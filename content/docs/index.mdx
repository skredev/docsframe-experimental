This is like shadcn/ui **NOT** a component library. It's a collection of re-usable components that you can copy and paste into your apps.

It primarily features components and templates `COMING SOON` contoured towards creating your personal **portfolio** page.

This library is heavily inspired by https://ui.shadcn.com and https://magicui.design.
